Packages

A package also known as a parcel is an object or group of objects contained in a paper or plastic to be shipped by post. They are commonly sent in cardboard boxes or plasic bubble wrap mailers and often used for e-commerce. The most popular courier companies include: FedEx, USPS, UPS, DHL, etc.


How to ship your own package


  1. Locate item(s) you'd like to send
  2. Package your item(s) neatly in a box or mailer and add protection if needed
  3. Write your own address on the top left of the parcel and your recipient's address in the center
  4. Bring the package to your chosen courier where the weight and dimensions will be taken to calculate the cost of shipping for your item
  5. Pay for the shipping and wait until your package gets to its destination!
